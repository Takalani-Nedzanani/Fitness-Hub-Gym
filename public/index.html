Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the Basic membership?

The Basic membership gives you access to the gym floor, all group fitness classes, and our locker room facilities.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my membership?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your membership at any time. Just speak to a member of our team at the front desk.

Is there a cancellation fee?

We offer flexible memberships. There is no cancellation fee, we just require 30 days notice.
